# ২. Authoritative logic: hazard thresholds, lead time, and warning signals

## Hazard Threshold

| Hazard component   | Low impact | Medium impact | High impact  | Very high impact |
| ------------------ | ---------- | ------------- | ------------ | ---------------- |
| Wind speed         | 62–88 km/h | 89–117 km/h   | 118–221 km/h | ≥222 km/h        |
| Storm surge height | <1.5 m     | 1.5–3.5 m     | 3.6–5.0 m    | >5.0 m           |
| 72-hour rainfall   | <150 mm    | 150–200 mm    | 200–250 mm   | >250 mm          |

---

## Lead Time Logic

| Lead time / likelihood           | Operational meaning                                                                                       | Suggested agent tone            |
| -------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------- |
| 36–48 hours (High likelihood)    | Hazard is highly likely and actions should move beyond awareness into preparation / relocation readiness. | Direct, urgent, but calm        |
| 48–72 hours (Medium likelihood)  | Hazard likely enough for pre-positioning, household protection, and shelter planning.                     | Preparatory and action-oriented |
| 72–120 hours (Low likelihood)    | Monitor closely; begin low-cost no-regret actions.                                                        | Advisory and early preparation  |
| >120 hours (Very low likelihood) | Regular monitoring only unless local conditions worsen.                                                   | Informational                   |

---

## CPP / BMD Signal

| Signal | Meaning | Agent Instruction |
|--------|---------|-------------------|
| 1–3 | Awareness | আপডেট অনুসরণ করুন |
| 4 | Warning | আশ্রয়কেন্দ্র নির্ধারণ করুন |
| 5–7 | Potential Impact | দ্রুত প্রস্তুতি নিন |
| 8–10 | Danger | দ্রুত আশ্রয়কেন্দ্র/নিরাপদ স্থানে সরে যান |

# ৫. Response Policy

| Rule                     | Operational guidance   |
| ------------------------ | ---------------------- |
| Keep it short            | 3–5 actions            |
| Use local plain language | Avoid technical jargon |
| Do not over-promise      | Use official sources   |
| Confirm understanding    | Ask a check question   |
| Escalate risk            | Flag urgent cases      |
